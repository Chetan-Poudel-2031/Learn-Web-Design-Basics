Learn to make a Simple Calculator!

A calculator is a vital tool for students as it enhances their efficiency in solving mathematical problems, allowing them to focus on understanding concepts rather than manual calculations. It helps in complex subjects like algebra, calculus, and statistics by reducing errors and saving time. Additionally, it fosters problem-solving skills by enabling students to explore advanced mathematical concepts with ease.

Here is the code to create a Simple Calculator using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. You can customize the design and functionality to suit your preferences and learning goals. Happy coding!
